Frequently Asked Questions

Why?

Power cost in the UK has got silly. I wanted to know the most efficient power supply in my budget, for my machine. The data is out there - the good folks at Cybenetics have done some great independent reporting - but it's buried in PDFs. The only information they didn't have that mattered to me was warranty - that's manually added, so there will be some gaps for now.

How often is the site updated?

The site is updated every 24 hours. Periodically, it will have trouble reaching amazon for prices - in that case, it may be a little longer between updates.

Why aren't there amazon.com/amazon.ca prices?

Amazon has a wonderful API for gathering prices, but I can't currently can't use it due to needing a number of referrals before they'll grant you access. As soon as I have access, I will update to use their API and offer more sites.

Where do you get your data?

Prices are grabbed from regional amazon sites

How do you make money?

I receive commissions for products purchased through the links on this site through Amazon's Affiliate Program]. This does not affect the price you pay.

Are you stealing or selling my data?

This site uses the least intrusive analytics I'm aware of. No cookies are stored, and very little data is sent. For more information, take a look at the privacy policy.

Will you add other sites for prices?

Maybe. This is a side project, that i'm just throwing out there to see what happens. If it sees some traffic, I'll dedicate some time to improvements.
